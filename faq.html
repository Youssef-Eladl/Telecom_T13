Telecommunication FAQ

1. What are the available service plans?

We offer prepaid, postpaid, and family plans to suit your needs. Each plan includes flexible data, calling, and SMS options.

2. How can I check my data balance?

You can check your data balance through our mobile app, SMS, or by dialing *123# from your phone.

3. What should I do if I experience network issues?

If you face network issues, please restart your device, check for outages in your area, or contact our customer support team for assistance.

4. How do I upgrade my current plan?

You can upgrade your plan through our website, mobile app, or by visiting your nearest service center.

5. Are international roaming options available?

Yes, we offer international roaming packages. Visit our website or contact customer service to activate roaming on your number.
